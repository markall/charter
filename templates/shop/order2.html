Why You Can Trust Us?

With 25 years worth of reputation at stake it is important to us that you can confidently buy from this site.
In order to do this we promise that:
  • Your details are encrypted and given only to those that require them to complete your order.

  • Once you place the order you will receive confirmation via email that the order has been placed.

  • The order will arrive at our sales desk where one of our sales team will check that it is correct. If something seems out of place or incomplete then they will contact you with their query.

  • Within 5 working days of you placing this order you will receive a delivery date, via email, for the week in which delivery can take place.

  • You will have a cooling off period of 7 days from placement of the order. Within this time the order can be cancelled.

  • The week of delivery you will be contacted by the driver he will expect help to unload the door. Our driver cannot unload on his own so please ensure you have help before ordering.

  • If on delivery you are not satisfied with your order then it can be returned at that time.

  • The items on this site have a manufacturers guarantee which can be as much as ten years
